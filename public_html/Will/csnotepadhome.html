Welcome to CSnotepad Virtual Receptionist and Virtual Office Services

We offer telephone call answering services designed by customers for customers. We are experts in affordable inbound and outbound call handling and professional virtual receptionist solutions. Our virtual receptionist solutions offer businesses, regardless of their size, an alternative way of handling their telephone calls and unlike the majority of our competitors we charge per call not per minute so you are not caught out with large unexpected bills.

We demonstrate the difference a team of enthusiastic and motivated staff can make to customer satisfaction and ultimately retention. We are not a call centre and so our customers are not simply numbers. We instead are a series of small teams each dedicated to a limited number of customers. As a result, each of our customers and their callers receive an outstanding personal service. Our customers also have their own account manager who is on hand to discuss and address their virtual receptionist needs whenever they want.

CSnotepad offers flexible and cost effective virtual receptionist solutions that provide businesses, no matter their size, with an alternative way of handling their telephone calls.

Our virtual receptionists are professional and skilled call handling agents who answer inbound telephone calls in your business name as an overflow or outsourced service. Our virtual receptionists can offer far more than just a message taking service as they can provide a range of virtual receptionist services such as call patching, appointment booking, order taking, etc…

Using a virtual receptionist can save your business time and money. With no training, breaks or holidays needed, your business can gain 68 hours of call handling cover per week, allowing for multiple inbound calls and a range of call handing skills at a fraction of the cost involved when employing full time receptionists.
